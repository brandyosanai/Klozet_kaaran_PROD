Products

Each product supports multiple images (3–10 typical). Click "Manage" to add, remove, reorder, or paste several image URLs at once. The first image is used as the thumbnail on Home and Collections; the full set shows as a gallery on the product page. Direct image links and Google Drive share links (file shared as "Anyone with the link") both work — Drive links convert automatically.

New Product

New products appear automatically on the Collections page. The Home page's featured sections are hand-curated, so a brand-new product won't show there until it's added to that page's HTML — but stock/price/sold-out status for anything already featured on Home stays in sync automatically.
